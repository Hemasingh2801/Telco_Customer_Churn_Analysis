# Telco Customer Churn Analysis
In this project, I didn’t just analyze churn using churn rate—because it only tells how many customers left.

By prioritizing potential revenue loss (CLTV – realized revenue), the focus shifted to which customers actually matter most, enabling smarter, high-impact retention decisions where quality of churn matters more than quantity.

# Executive Summary
Customer churn was concentrated in a few predictable high-risk segments, with early-tenure Month-to-Month customers showing the highest churn and potential revenue loss. 

Price, service, and experience were not major churn factors in isolation;however, when combined with early-tenure Month-to-Month customers, they significantly increased churn and created the most vulnerable segment.
